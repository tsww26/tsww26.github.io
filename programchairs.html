Program Committee

We are grateful to the following esteemed researchers who have confirmed their participation as members of the Program Committee (PC).

  • Joseph Kwarteng, The Open University, UK
  • Paula Reyero Lobo, Manchester University, UK
  • Maria do Mar Vau, University College London, UK
  • Pieter Heyvaert, Ghent University, Belgium
  • Daryna Dementieva, TUM School of Computation, Information and Technology, Germany
  • María Estrella Vallecillo Rodríguez, Universidad de Jaén, Spain

The majority of our committee members bring a strong technology-focused perspective, enriched by insights from fields such as social sciences, law, and psychology. This interdisciplinary foundation enables us to engage effectively with diverse submissions and foster meaningful dialogue on addressing online violence against women and girls.
